Ender Dragon Pet

The Ender Dragon Pet is a Stone Sword Combat Pet obtained
as a rare drop from Ender Dragons in the Dragon's Nest,
requiring 450 Dragon Weight to drop. The Ender Dragon pet
has 2 rarities, EPIC and
LEGENDARY.
Base level Stats
  • Strength: 0.5
  • Crit Damage: +0.5%
  • Crit Chance: +0.1%
